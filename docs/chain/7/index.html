Has information you consumed online changed how you feel about a group of people in the last year?

Changed feelings aren't the problem. Feelings should change as we learn. The question is: did you learn from diverse, verified sources? Or did you learn from an algorithm optimizing for your engagement?

One is education. The other is conditioning.
